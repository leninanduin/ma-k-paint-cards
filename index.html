Maschinen Krieger Printable Paint Cards

Printable paint cards for Maschinen Krieger models.

This cards are intented to help modelers to test color schemes before paint is applied to the model.

Feel free to reuse any file, if you are interested in contribute to this database send me your files to ma.k.paint.cards@gmail.com.
